curlshell.io

Don't try this at home.
curl2shell.io is a restful microservice that allows users to do terrible things to their computers without having to decide for themselves which terrible thing they wish to do.

Get Started!

curl -H "x-raw-command:1" http://curl2shell.io/v1/command/1 | bash 

Provide a command ID to select a specific command, or leave it out to get a
completely random command.

Note: Some commands may be harmful/destructive to your computer. Handle with care.
